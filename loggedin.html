A-H

  • Ben and Holly's Little Kingdom

    Meet Ben Elf, Princess Holly, King and Queen Thistle, and all the mystical, magical elves and fantastic creatures that inhabit the Little Kingdom. Kids will learn that they too have the magic inside and will join in on the fun and adventure.


    Blaze and the Monster Machines

    Blaze and the Monster Machines features Blaze, the world's most amazing monster truck, and his driver, AJ. Preschoolers help Blaze on his adventures in Axle City, solving problems based on a STEM curriculum (science, technology, engineering, math).


    Blue's Clues

    Blue's Clues is a play-along, think-along series featuring hosts, Steve or Joe, and an energetic puppy named Blue. In each episode, Joe or Steve invites viewers into a computer-animated storybook world to look for clues and solve the day's puzzle.


    Bubble Guppies

    Dive into a watery world of learning and music with the Bubble Guppies! This hilarious, variety-style kids show teaches preschoolers science, math, and reading while it explores themes such as dinosaurs, recycling, rock 'n roll, colors, and cowboys.


    Dino Dan

    Dino Dan is an ordinary 10 year-old boy with an extraordinary ability. He eats, sleeps, and breathes dinosaurs and has a funny way of seeing dinosaurs in any situation. The show introduces kids to the scientific method in a fun and engaging way.


    Dora & Friends: Into the City

    Armed with a magical bracelet, a trusted Map App, and strength that comes from smart and loving friends Alana, Emma, Naiya, Kate, and Pablo, Dora invites preschoolers to solve problems, speak Spanish, give back to the community--and help save the day!


    Dora the Explorer

    Join Dora, Boots, Backpack, Map, and Swiper for interactive preschool adventures in your backyard and around the world. Preschoolers can sing and dance and learn math, reading, Spanish words, and more as they help Dora on her adventures.


    Fresh Beat Band of Spies

    It's time to spy it up! Super spies Twist, Shout, Kiki, and Marina (along with Bo Monkey) use their individual talents to outwit a goofy cast of bad guys. Watch as they use the coolest spy gadgets to solve an endless series of wild mysteries.


    Go, Diego, Go!

    Go, Diego, Go is on a mission to help rescue animals in trouble. Using observation skills and scientific tools like a field journal and camera, Diego introduces information about each animal's sound, movement, habitat, diet, family, and characteristics.



I-P

  • Julius Jr.

    Enter the world of funky monkey Julius Jr., Worry Bear, Clancy, Sheree, and Ping where ordinary objects come to life and fantastic adventures are just a door away. Julius Jr. proves the best inventions are the ones that help your friends.


    Lalaloopsy

    This animated series stars friends who were magically brought to life once their last stitch was sewn. The lovable characters Mittens, Jewel, Spot, and Dot are all unique. The friends comes together for "fun-tastical" adventures in a whimsical world.


    Little Charmers

    Charmers-in-training Hazel and her two best pals, Posie and Lavender, try to learn magical powers and go on exciting adventures. Kids feel confident that they too have the magic inside and can do anything they set their hearts and minds to.


    Max & Ruby

    Meet two funny bunny siblings, the energetic and mischievous Max, and the patient, smart and goal-oriented Ruby. The show models empowering messages by showing Max and Ruby playing together and resolving their differences respectfully and supportively.


    Mia and Me

    Mia receives a book and a bracelet that transports her to the magical storybook world of Centopia, where she's transformed into a flying elf with the ability to talk to unicorns. Join Mia and friends in the quest to save the unicorns from an evil queen!


    Mike the Knight

    Welcome to the world of Mike the Knight! Each action-packed episode transports kids to a world filled with missions, castles, dragons, Vikings, and trolls. The show encourages independence, problem-solving, self-confidence, and imaginative play in kids.


    PAW Patrol

    PAW Patrol stars a pack of pups--Chase, Marshall, Rocky, Zuma, Rubble, and Skye--all led by a boy named Ryder. The PAW Patrol goes on high-stakes rescue missions to protect Adventure Bay while teaching kids how to solve problems through teamwork.


    Peppa Pig

    Peppa Pig is an energetic piggy who lives with Mummy, Daddy, and little brother George. She loves to jump in mud puddles and make loud snorting noises. Episodes feature fun, everyday activities that support kids' social & emotional development.


    Peter Rabbit

    Welcome to the magical, timeless world of Peter Rabbit. His taste for radishes and thirst for adventure propel Peter and friends Benjamin and Lily through action-packed journeys that thrill preschoolers, teaching them to be optimistic and resilient.



Q-Z

  • Shimmer and Shine

    Join Shimmer and Shine, twin genies-in-training, as they grant wishes for their best friend, Leah. The wishes don't always go as planned, leaving them with a magical mess to clean up! By working together the trio solves problems to make things right again!


    Team Umizoomi

    It’s Team Umizoomi to the rescue! Join Milli, Geo, Bot, and UmiCar to help solve everyday problems in Umi City. Preschoolers will need to use their "Mighty Math Powers" like shapes, counting, sorting, measuring, and patterns to help the team.


    The Backyardigans

    The Backyardigans features five spirited friends, Uniqua, Pablo, Tyrone, Tasha, & Austin. Their vivid imaginations take them on epic musical journeys in their own backyard. Kids can sing, dance, and stretch their own imaginations while watching the show.


    The Fresh Beat Band

    Get up off your feet and jam out with the Fresh Beats! The Fresh Beat Band inspires kids to get active with dance and teaches musical terms and music appreciation. The four best friends Twist, Shout, Marina, and Kiki also model problem-solving and teamwork in every episode!


    Tickety Toc

    Tickety Toc is a magical place filled with wonderfully odd characters and slapstick surprises. Twins Tommy and Tallulah race against time to make it to the Tickety Clock's hourly chime time, demonstrating teamwork and problem-solving along the way.


    Wallykazam!

    Get word-wise with Wallykazam! Join Wally, Bobgoblin, Norville and friends for adventures in the world of words and reading. Watch as words come to life with the wave of Wally’s magic stick. Anything is possible with Wally--all he needs is a word!


    Wonder Pets!

    Watch Linny, Tuck, and Ming-Ming as they learn, play, and save the day! This groundbreaking musical show is not only about teamwork, the Wonder Pets teaches kids about sharing, helping others, and being a good friend.


    Yo Gabba Gabba!

    Join DJ Lance Rock and his friendly toy monster friends Muno, Foofa, Brobee, Plex, and Toodee. Yo Gabba Gabba invites preschoolers to sing and dance along to a variety of catchy songs that impart simple yet important life lessons.


    Zack and Quack

    Enter exciting new worlds and have amazing voyages unfold before your eyes. Zack & Quack is an animated series full of fantastical surprises set inside the most amazing pop-up book ever, where pulling each tab brings exciting and unexpected things.
